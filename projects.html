Memento Mortis

Memento Mortis is a dark, atmospheric game developed in Unreal Engine 5, blending horror and suspense with rich storytelling. Players navigate eerie environments, facing both psychological and physical challenges, with an emphasis on choices that impact the outcome

  • 3D Modeling: Custom assets and environments that bring the Maze of Eternity and its horrors to life.
  • Game Scripting: Utilized Unreal Engine's Blueprint and C++ for game logic, traps, puzzles, and interactive elements.
  • Sound Design: Atmospheric audio enhances tension and builds suspense.
  • Gameplay Mechanics: Combines exploration, survival elements, and combat mechanics, featuring a hidden weapon.

ObstaBall

Obstaball is an exciting endless runner game that challenges players to navigate through a continuously generated landscape filled with obstacles. Developed using C# and Unity, this game offers a fast-paced experience that tests players' reflexes and timing..

  • Endless Gameplay: The procedurally generated levels ensure that no two runs are the same, keeping players engaged and challenged.
  • Simple Controls: Easy-to-learn controls make it accessible for players of all ages, allowing them to jump and dodge obstacles with precision.

ECOIQ: True or False

Developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2024 by Orbiting Novices, ECOIQ: True or False is an educational trivia game that raises awareness about environmental issues through interactive true/false questions. The game is based on the GLOBE Protocol, encouraging players to learn about and investigate global and local environmental challenges.
