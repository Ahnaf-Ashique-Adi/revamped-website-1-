Hi, I’m Ahnaf Ashique Adi, a passionate computer science and engineering student at BRAC University. My journey in tech began with a fascination for game development, leading to the creation of games like Memento Mortis, and ObstaBall. I enjoy experimenting with new technologies and am always eager to learn and take on new challenges.

You’ll often find me in a mix of roles—whether it’s coding in my workspace, attending events like the NASA Space Apps Challenge , or taking on DevOps projects. One of my recent projects involves setting up a personal cloud server using Nextcloud, which has expanded my knowledge in Linux and server management.

When I’m not working on development, I like to engage in collaborative projects, such as ECOIQ: True or False, a trivia game made during the NASA Space Apps Challenge with my team, Orbiting Novices. It’s always fulfilling to create something impactful while learning and growing with a team.

You’ll see me in a professional setting in one of the photos here, but I’m also sharing a more casual side, showing the person behind the projects. I believe in keeping a balance between professionalism and being approachable.
In the future, I aspire to use my DevOps skills to start my own tech startup. Feel free to check out my projects or connect with me on my social media.
